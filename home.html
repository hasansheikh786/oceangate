Introduction To Ocean Gate

OceanGate, Inc. was an American privately owned company based
in Everett, Washington, that provided crewed submersibles for
tourism, industry, research, and exploration. The company was
founded in 2009 by "Stockton Rush" and "Guillermo Söhnlein".

Submersibles

ANTIPODES
304 METERS (997 FEET)

The Antipodes Submarine is a cutting-edge underwater
vessel engineered for deep-sea exploration and research.
Featuring an innovative design optimized for efficiency and performance, the Antipodes is capable of reaching extreme depths while maintaining stability and safety.

CYCLOPS
500 METERS (1,640 FEET)

The Cyclops Submarine is a state-of-the-art underwater
vehicle engineered for deep-sea exploration and
research missions. Its sleek and compact design allows
it to maneuver efficiently through challenging underwater
environments while maintaining stability and safety.

TITAN
4,000 METERS (13,123 FEET)

The Titan Submarine is a compact and versatile underwater vehicle designed for exploration in deep-sea environments. With its robust construction and advanced technology, the Titan can withstand extreme pressures and temperatures found in the depths of the ocean.

About Us

"A Story of Loss and Continuation"

  • OceanGate, Inc., headquartered in Everett, Washington, offered crewed submersibles for various purposes.
  • Founded in 2009 by Stockton Rush and Guillermo Söhnlein, it acquired the vessel Antipodes and built Cyclops 1 and Titan.
  • OceanGate initiated Titanic wreck tours in 2021, with a ticket price of US$250,000 per person by 2022.
  • Tragically, Titan imploded during a voyage to the Titanic site on June 18, 2023, resulting in the loss of all five occupants, including Rush.
  • An international search and rescue operation was launched, discovering the wreckage on June 22.
  • Following the incident, OceanGate's Everett office closed indefinitely on June 21, and operations were suspended on July 6./li>
  • Despite the setback, as of February 2024, OceanGate, Inc. remains an active entity
